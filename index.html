Welcome
Have you ever wondered how life became from being an adventure to a day to day drudgery? How the rich get richer every year but the poor are scratching to get by, even in the so called First World? The answer is simple: Money is created as debt, the interest is never created, and so the debt increases and the interests gets paid upwards to the holders and lenders of money. Time to end this.

Every kind of money system that has been tried has failed and has created countless misery. A fixed money supply not backed by gold is controlled by the issuer. A gold backed money system can be easily cornered. And a money-as-debt system backed by a central bank like we have today is an unescapable problem and enslaves the entire world. The solution? Work for free, for the good of your fellow man.

To take care of your fellow man really doesn't even fit the definition of work. But to feed the almost 8 billion people on this planet, to clothe them and to sustain their needs, we do need to come together. Let life be an adventure again by living for today and giving the earth a much needed rest. If we all leave yesterday behind, then what's to stop us from negating the collective debt and just starting anew? Only our own free will and human nature to make sure our fellow man is safe and taken care of is important. The rest is history, literally
